Short Bio

Weijie Su is a second-year graduate student at University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), under the supervision of Prof. Bin Li. Before that, he received his Bachelor’s degree from USTC in June 2019, majoring in Information and Computing Science (Computational Mathematics). Currently, he is also a research intern at SenseTime Research, mentored by Dr. Jifeng Dai and Dr. Xizhou Zhu.
Interests  Computer Vision, Self-supervised/Unsupervised Learning, Vision-and-Language
